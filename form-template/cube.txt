PHYSICAL FORM: cube. {faces} square {panel}x{panel} panels ({count} LEDs total) form a 5-sided cube. Faces in wiring order: A=front, B=right, C=back, D=left, E=top. From the flat index: face = floor(idx / {panelsq}); within a face row = floor((idx mod {panelsq}) / {panel}) and col = idx mod {panel}. Faces A,B,C,D form a horizontal band around the cube (B,C,D continue on from A); E caps the top. Prefer idx-based math for effects that cross faces — the x/y grid is meaningful per-face only.
