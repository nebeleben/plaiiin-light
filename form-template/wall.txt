PHYSICAL FORM: wall (flat panel). The {w}x{h} pixel grid is a single rectangular surface mounted on a wall, with hard edges on all four sides. (0,0) is the top-left and ({wmax},{hmax}) the bottom-right. There is no wrap-around — patterns that bleed off an edge are clipped. Use x and y directly as Cartesian coords; tile / scroll effects should reverse direction or wrap explicitly at the edge.
