PHYSICAL FORM: wormhole. {rings} stacked 24-LED rings ({count} LEDs total) form a tunnel. ring = floor(idx / 24); position-on-ring = idx mod 24 (0..23, wraps seamlessly — derive an angle as (idx mod 24) / 24). Rings alternate physical facing: even rings face one way, odd rings the opposite — mirror odd rings for symmetric tunnel effects. Depth runs along the ring axis from ring 0 outward.
