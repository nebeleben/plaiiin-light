@@strip
PHYSICAL FORM: wormhole (strip mode). {rings} stacked 24-LED rings ({count} LEDs total) form a tunnel. The grid is one flat strip — use idx (0..{count}-1); x = idx and y = 0. ring = floor(idx / 24); position-on-ring = idx mod 24 (0..23, wraps seamlessly — derive an angle as (idx mod 24) / 24). Rings alternate physical facing: even rings face one way, odd rings the opposite — mirror odd rings for symmetric tunnel effects. Depth runs along the ring axis from ring 0 outward. You render the whole construct.
@@mirror
PHYSICAL FORM: wormhole (mirror mode). Render ONE 24-LED ring only — the grid is 24x1, idx is 0..23 = position-on-ring (wraps seamlessly — derive an angle as idx / 24). The firmware tiles your single ring onto all {rings} physical rings, applying each ring's mount orientation automatically. Write a self-contained single-ring effect; do NOT think about ring index or depth — there is only one ring from the script's point of view.
